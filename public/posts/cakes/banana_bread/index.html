Banana Bread

cakes    banana / bread / cake / sweet / brunch / breakfast
2021-1-16 18:08

Ingredients:

Dry

  • 2 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/3-3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
Wet
  • 2 overripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/2 cup soy milk
  • 3/4 cup oil
  • 1 butter vanilla essence, or 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp rhum
Topping
  • 3/4 cup chopped pecans and/or walnuts
  • 1-2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 1 tbsp cold vegan butter

How to:

  1. Mix the wet and dry ingredients together in separate bowls
  2. Combine them and fold the batter, and do not overmix so that it doesn’t form a glutinous dough. Use a spatula rather than a whisk
  3. Mix the topping ingredients together with your hands
  4. Pour the batter in a greased mold, and sprinkle the topping
  5. Bake at 180C for 40-50 mins, until a knife comes out clean. Don’t be like me and not check, do check!!
