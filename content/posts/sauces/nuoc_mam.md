#### Ingredients

* 1-2 cloves chopped garlic
* 2 tsp chili flakes, or fresh chili
* 0.5 tsp salt
* 2 tbsp maple syrup
* 1 tbsp lime juice
* 125 (0.5 c) hot water

#### How to

1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl, until sugar and salt have disolved
